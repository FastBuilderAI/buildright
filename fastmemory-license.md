# FastMemory Engine: Revenue-Based Licensing (Commercial)

This document specifies the commercial licensing terms for the **FastMemory Cognitive Engine**, which powers the BuildRight engineering layer.

## 🏆 Licensing Model

The FastMemory engine is free for individuals and small enterprises, and revenue-scaled for large organizations.

### 1. Community License ($0)
**Eligibility**:
- Individuals (Developers, Students, Researchers).
- Open Source Projects.
- **Enterprises with < $20,000,000 Annual Revenue**.

### 2. Enterprise License (Revenue-Scaled)
**Eligibility**:
- **Enterprises with > $20,000,000 Annual Revenue**.
- Organizations requiring specialized production support, air-gapped sync, or HIPAA/SOC2 compliance certification.

## 🤝 Relationship with BuildRight
While the **BuildRight Engineering Layer** (the 131-framework ontology and associated scripts) is 100% MIT Open Source, the high-performance **FastMemory Engine** used to cluster and retrieve logic is subject to this revenue-based model.
